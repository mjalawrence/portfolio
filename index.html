About Me

I’m a recent graduate of Eyup Sheffield on the iO Academy where I studied to become a full stack developer. I have always enjoyed using computers to create and am now excited to see where they can take me in my career.

After spending most of my twenties enjoying a different way of life abroad, I have returned to the UK motivated to settle in, learn and develop professionally.

Keep scrolling to see a selection of the projects I completed at the academy as part of a scrum team utilising a variety of stacks and technologies.

Projects

Food Delivered

A food-ordering app built with React.js that accessed an API of restaurants offering a variety of meal options and allowed the user to place an order.

The functionality of this app includes filtering food options, the ability to add and delete from the order, and live displaying of order total. Working in the React.js framework left a strong impression of the value of separation of concerns as a coding principle.
